Eye and Amoeba – Video & Reels Platform
1️⃣ Structure
menu.html: Main page showing all videos and reels.
video1.html, video2.html…: Video pages (local MP4 or YouTube) with full controls.
reels.html: Instagram Reels-style page with interactive comments and animated Badge.
b.css: Global styling for the project.
b.js: Controls for videos (Replay, Next, Speed, Badge).
Particles.js: Dynamic animated background.
2️⃣ Core Features
Play local videos, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels.
Control buttons: Replay ⏪, Next ⏭, playback speed, video rating.
Animated Badge shows on rating or interaction.
Interactive comment pop-ups for reels.
Glow and gradient effects with cinematic-style visuals.
Smooth navigation between videos and pages.
3️⃣ Technologies
HTML5: <video> and <iframe>.
CSS3: Glow, shadows, gradients, animations.
JS: Video control, Badge, comments, navigation.
Particles.js: Animated and engaging background.
4️⃣ Creative Touch
Futuristic, visually appealing design with social media-like experience.
Smooth user interaction, with dynamic engagement for every video or reel.
